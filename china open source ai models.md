## 6. Learning Notes (Simplified)
- **Knowledge distillation:** training a smaller AI to behave like a bigger one.  
- **NeRF (Neural Radiance Fields):** tech that converts images into 3D scenes with realistic lighting.  
- **Gaussian Splatting:** a newer, faster method to reconstruct 3D scenes from photos.  
- **Multimodal models:** AIs that understand text, images, audio, and video together.  
- **Permissive licenses (MIT, Apache 2.0):** safe for commercial use.
