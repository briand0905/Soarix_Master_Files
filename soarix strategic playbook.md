# 2. SOFTWARE FACTORY MODEL (THE 4 FACTORIES)

## PRODUCT FACTORY
Purpose: Turn ideas into usable digital products  
Roles: PM, Designer, Frontend, Backend, Data Engineering  
Tools: Notion, Miro, Figma, GitHub, Cursor, Replit  
Output: Working product

## DELIVERY FACTORY
Purpose: Make products stable, scalable, secure  
Roles: DevOps, SRE, QA, Cybersecurity  
Tools: AWS/GCP, Docker, Kubernetes, Cloudflare, Grafana, Sentry  
Output: Deployment, reliability, uptime, performance

## GROWTH FACTORY
Purpose: User acquisition, revenue, brand  
Roles: Marketing, Sales, SEO, Ads, Community  
Tools: Webflow, HubSpot, Zapier, Meta/Google Ads, SEMrush  
Output: Customers, MRR, growth

## AI FACTORY (Overlay)
Purpose: Intelligence, automation, personalization, agents  
Roles: AI Architect, ML Engineer, Data Scientist  
Tools: OpenAI, Gemini, Claude, LangChain, LlamaIndex, Pinecone  
Output: AI-powered systems
